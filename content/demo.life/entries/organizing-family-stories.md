Collections can gather related entries into a curated path through an archive.

For family history, that could mean stories about people, scanned documents, photographs, and notes from interviews. In this demo, the collection is simpler, but the structure is the same.

The archive stays portable because relationships are stored as plain ids in frontmatter.
